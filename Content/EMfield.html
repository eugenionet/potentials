Electro-Magnetic Field (force caused by potential)

  • static: lovslized source charge at rest, only spacial dependency, no time dependency
  • stationary: source charce at uniform movement, steady current flow, space and time dependency
  • dynamic: source charge at fast movement, radiation, space and time dependency
  • relativistc: source charge at very speedy movement, radiation, space and time dependency


  • rest localization vs. time-dependency
  • slow vs. fast movemnt
  • vacuum vs. matter
  • local vs. global of \(\mathscr{B}\), singularity vs. regularity of \(\mathscr{E}\)
  • macro vs. micro
  • far-field vs. near-field


Magnetic Field in Vacuum

\[\begin{split} & \mathscr{B} = \nabla \times \boldsymbol{U_m} \coloneqq rot(A) \newline & \stackrel{\textnormal{Matter}}{=} \mu_0 \left( \mathscr{H} + \mathscr{M} \right) \newline & \stackrel{\textnormal{Vacuum}}{=} \frac{1}{c} \left( \frac{r}{\left| r \right|} \times \mathscr{E} \right) \newline & \stackrel{\textnormal{Far Field}}{=} \frac{1}{2} \frac{1}{2\pi} \mu_0 \frac{k^2 e^{i(kr - \omega t)}}{r} \frac{r}{\left| r \right|} \times \left( c p_{0_{dipol}} + m_{0_{dipol}} \times \frac{r}{\left| r \right|} \right) \newline & \stackrel{\textnormal{Near Field}}{\approx} \frac{1}{2} \frac{1}{2\pi} \mu_0 q \frac{v \times r}{\left| r \right|^2} \approx \frac{1}{2} \frac{1}{2\pi} i k c \mu_0 \frac{ \frac{r}{\left| r \right|} \times p_m}{r^2} \end{split} \tag{1}\]

Magnetic Field in Matter

Exitation \(\hat{=}\) Displacement \[\begin{split} & \text{Exication:} \qquad \mathscr{H} = \frac{1}{\mu_0}\mathscr{B} - \mathscr{M} = \frac{1}{\mu}\mathscr{B} \newline & \text{Magnetisation:} \qquad \mathscr{M} = \frac{\mu - \mu_0}{\mu\mu_0}\mathscr{B} = \frac{\mu - \mu_0}{\mu_0}\mathscr{H} \end{split} \tag{2}\]

Electric Field in Vacuum

\[\begin{split} & \mathscr{E} \stackrel{\textnormal{conservativ}}{=} -\nabla U_e \stackrel{\textnormal{dynamic}}{=} -\nabla U_e - \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \boldsymbol{U_m} \newline & \stackrel{\textnormal{fast moving charge}}{=} \newline & \frac{1}{2} \frac{1}{2\pi} \frac{1}{\varepsilon_0} \frac{1}{r} \frac{1}{\left( 1 - \frac{r}{\left| r \right|} \cdot \frac{v}{c} \right)^3 } q \left[ \underbrace{ \left( 1 - \frac{v^2}{c^2} \right) \frac{\frac{r}{\left| r \right|} - \frac{v}{c}}{r} }_\text{uniform movement} + \underbrace{ \frac{\frac{r}{\left| r \right|} \times \left[ \left( \frac{r}{\left| r \right|} - \frac{v}{c} \right) \times \dot{v} \right] }{c^2} }_\text{accelerated movement} \right] \newline & \stackrel{\textnormal{Far Field}} = c \mathscr{B} \times \frac{1}{\left| r \right|} \newline & \stackrel{\textnormal{Near Field}} \approx \frac{1}{2} \frac{1}{2\pi} \frac{1}{\varepsilon_0} q \frac{r}{\left| r \right|^3} \approx \frac{1}{2} \frac{1}{2\pi} \frac{1}{\varepsilon_0} \frac{ 3 \frac{r}{\left| r \right|} \cdot p_m \frac{r}{\left| r \right|} - p_m}{r^3} \end{split} \tag{3}\]
